# ECE_CfileSplit
The first step to using our program is entering your three digit account number. The valid account #s that we have stored in our banking system's account struct are 101, 102, and 103. If your account is found, you will be prompted to enter your four-digit PIN, which will be checked with the correct PIN in our account struct to verify that you are the owner of the account. The PIN for account 101 is 1234, the PIN for account 102 is 4321, and the PIN for account 103 is 1111. If the correct PIN is entered, the bank menu will be presented. User will enter a number 1-5 that corresponds to their choice of action (Deposit, Withdraw, Check Balance, Transfer money, Exit).
